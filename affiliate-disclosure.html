How we make money

Some links on ToolStackChoice.com are affiliate links. This means that if you click a link and then sign up for or purchase a product, we may receive a commission from the vendor at no extra cost to you. These links are marked with rel="sponsored" in the HTML for transparency with search engines.

Our editorial independence

Affiliate relationships do not influence our reviews, scores, or recommendations. We review tools on their own merits using a consistent methodology. We will always tell you if a tool is not the right fit for your use case, even if we have an affiliate relationship with that vendor. If a better non-affiliated tool exists for your situation, we will recommend it.

In accordance with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission’s guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, we disclose any material connection between ToolStackChoice.com and the tools we recommend. This page, combined with the rel="sponsored" attribute on affiliate links, constitutes our disclosure.
